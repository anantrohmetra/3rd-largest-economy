# Religion — Communal Violence, Hate Crime & Minority Rights

## Muslims

- 84% of victims of cow vigilante violence between 2010 and 2017 were Muslim.
- In the 2002 Gujarat riots, approximately 1,000 people were killed — the majority Muslim. Around 20,000 homes were destroyed.
- 90% of religion-based hate crimes between 2009 and 2019 occurred after the BJP came to power in 2014.
- In 2024: 59 communal riots recorded — 13 lives claimed, 10 of them Muslim.
- Source: [Cow Vigilante Violence — Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cow_vigilante_violence_in_India)
- Source: [2002 Gujarat Riots — Ballard Brief](https://ballardbrief.byu.edu/issue-briefs/the-2002-gujarat-riots)
- Source: [Religious Hate Crimes — South Asia Justice Campaign](https://www.southasiajusticecampaign.org)
- Source: [India Communal Violence 2024 — CSSS](https://csss-isla.com)

## Christians

- 1 Christian killed in documented 2023 hate crime incidents.
- 1 Christian killed in 2024 mob lynching incidents.
- Source: [Religious Violence 2023 — South Asia Justice Campaign](https://www.southasiajusticecampaign.org)

## Cow Vigilante Violence

- At least 44 people killed by cow vigilantes between May 2015 and December 2018.
- 36 of those 44 victims were Muslim. Approximately 280 people were injured in 100+ attacks.
- 97% of all cow vigilante attacks on record occurred after May 2014 — when the BJP came to national power.
- Source: [44 Killed by Cow Vigilantes — The Print](https://theprint.in/india/44-killed-by-cow-vigilantes-since-2015/153031/)
- Source: [Cow Violence Tracker — IndiaSpend](https://www.indiaspend.com/cow-violence/)
- Source: [Violent Cow Protection in India — Human Rights Watch, 2019](https://www.hrw.org/report/2019/02/19/violent-cow-protection-india)

## BJP & Hate Speech

- "Either prepare to die or get ready to kill." — Rally speech attributed to BJP-affiliated speaker.
- "Even if 100 of us kill 20 lakhs of them, we will be victorious." — Rally speech.
- Documented hate speech in India increased by nearly 500% from 2014 to 2018.
- BJP MLA Haribhushan Thakur Bachaul: "Muslims should be set ablaze like Ravana effigies."
- BJP leader Dinesh Kushwaha assaulted 65-year-old Bhawarlal Jain, mistaking him for a Muslim. Jain died.
- Source: [90% Hate Crimes Post-2014 — FactChecker.in](https://www.factchecker.in)
- Source: [Islamophobia in India — Ballard Brief](https://ballardbrief.byu.edu/issue-briefs/the-2002-gujarat-riots)
- Source: [BJP MLA Hate Speech — The Wire](https://thewire.in)

## Hunger & Survival

- India's 2023 Global Hunger Index score is 28.7 — classified as **serious**.
- 74% of India's population cannot afford a healthy diet.
- 39% of India's population falls short of a nutrient-adequate diet.
- Source: [Global Hunger Index 2023 — India](https://www.globalhungerindex.org/india.html)
- Source: [UN SOFI Report 2024 — FAO](https://www.fao.org/publications/sofi)
